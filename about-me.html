Hi there ...

I am looking to change careers from a registered nurse to a full-stack developer. I went to Kenya Medical Training College for my nursing career path. Worked as one for a few years and realized that I needed to find something that is more in tune with my strengths and personality. The idea of programming came from someone who knows me very well and they were right. My first foray into learning how to program was with the CS50 Introduction To Computer Science course by Harvard. I was hooked.

It has been a continuous learning process since I started. It hasn't always been enjoyable but it has been, and continues to be, worth it as evidenced by my GitHub account on the Contact Me page. I have some other back-end and front-end courses under my belt from Udemy and other independent teachers like Mosh Hamedani. There are some personal projects that I have built to enhance my skills and solidify the lessons that I am learning. A few of these are available on the Projects page.

Having a background in the medical world and personal experiences as a patient, I want to eventually be able to provide some innovative solutions to some queries that I've noticed. These can hopefully be practical, effective and helpful to people.
